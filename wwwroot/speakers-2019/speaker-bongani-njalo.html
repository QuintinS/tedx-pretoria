Talk Title
Abafana abafani - Young men are not the same

Bio

I am queer. I am Xhosa. I am an artist. I am a son. I am a brother. I am a change agent. I am all of these things at once and they cannot be looked at in isolation.

A short overview of the talk

My talk will focus on the birth and provenance of my intervention, Abafana abafani which was a defiant public celebration of the diversity of masculinity in Xhosa culture. I birthed this performance in Port Elizabeth, where the most violent protests took place in response to the movie Inxeba (The Wound), which is the Eastern Cape provinceâ€™s most globally celebrated cultural export. The movie has not been circulated or shown in the Eastern Cape due to rampant homophobia in the province.
