Jayshree Naidoo

Twitter Handle
@Jayshnaidoo
Talk Title
People are more than the jobs they do

Bio

Jayshree has held senior roles in some of South Africa’s largest companies and has never been one to shy away from challenging the status quo if it did not align with her purpose.

A short overview of the talk

Jayshree’s talk is about recognising that people are so much more than their jobs or titles. She has first-hand experience of how large corporates can destroy your spirit, if you don’t stand up for yourself and your beliefs. She thinks it's time to challenge the system and ensure that people in positions of power use their influence for good, to grow and nurture those around them, instead of breaking them down through corporate bullying and politics.
