Bio

I am a connector. A communicator. A purpose-driven, inspired inspirer. Often referred to as ‘The Beat,’ I am a proudly South African entrepreneur who also co-owns SummerTime Creatives. I have established a personal brand through which I offer a broad range of consultative services. Mentoring. Training. Facilitation. Emceeing. Coaching. Creating. And building. I am energised by contributing to people’s, brands’ and businesses’ transformation and growth journeys. The annual TEDxPretoria event is the perfect playground to meet and connect with like-minded, idea-generating, rain-making people!

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxPretoria, where x = independently organized TED event.

At our TEDxPretoria event, TEDTalks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.
